Yeah, I mess around with pixel art sometimes. It’s kind of like pressing pause on the chaos and capturing moments in tiny, blocky details. It’s nostalgic but also very... distorted. - Why this design?

Because clean portfolios feel like corporate resumes dressed up in CSS. This? This feels like a hidden terminal you weren’t supposed to find. It’s more "personal ghost in the machine" than "hire me please." If you understand it, you’re probably in the right place. If you don’t... maybe that’s the point.
